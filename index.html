Who we are

A Foundation Built on Compassion

Established in 2018, Zimbabwe Future Foundation International (ZFFI) is a community-based organisation with an unwavering commitment to improving the social welfare of vulnerable people across Zimbabwe.

We believe that sustainable change begins within communities. ZFFI channels the energy of Zimbabwe's youth and the generosity of its partners into programmes that create lasting transformation — from malnutrition relief to scholarship pathways.

ZFFI welcomes partnerships and support from individuals, organisations, and institutions who share our commitment to building a more equitable Zimbabwe.

"Every child is precious. Every family deserves hope. We exist to make that hope tangible — one community at a time."

MBM

Chinese Medical Partnership

ZFFI has engaged with Chinese medical practitioners and institutions committed to delivering healthcare outreach to underserved communities in Zimbabwe, extending specialist services and medical supplies to rural areas.

University of Zimbabwe Medical Students

Since ZFFI's very first outreach in 2018 in Beatrice, Ward 17, voluntary medical students from the University of Zimbabwe have been vital partners — many of whom are now qualified medical professionals.
